== Description ==

The Free Kindergarten WordPress Theme is a versatile, beautifully crafted theme tailored to meet the unique needs of kindergartens, preschools, and nursery schools. Designed with early childhood education in mind, this theme is perfect for showcasing all the essential elements a kindergarten has to offer. From the curriculum and daily activities to teacher profiles and learning programs, everything can be presented in an organised and engaging manner. Schools that want to build a strong online presence to attract parents and share key information, such as classroom activities and early learning resources, will find this theme especially beneficial. Being free, it provides a cost-effective solution for schools and educators who may be working with limited budgets but still want to maintain a professional and user-friendly website to promote their services. The clean and colourful design creates an inviting digital environment that appeals to parents seeking a safe, engaging, and nurturing space for their children.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
